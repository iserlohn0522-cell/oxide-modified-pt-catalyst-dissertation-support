| Dependency | Role | Release treatment | Terms checked |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ultralytics YOLOv8m-seg base checkpoint | Detector initialization and architecture | Link/reference only; the dissertation-trained checkpoint is a separate release asset | Ultralytics AGPL-3.0 licensing page, checked 2026-07-17 |
